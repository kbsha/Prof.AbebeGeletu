About

Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu is the German Research Chair at AIMS Rwanda. His research focuses on systems optimization for sustainable resource utilization in Africa, multidisciplinary engineering research, and AI- and data-driven approaches for complex real-world problems.

Prior to joining AIMS Rwanda in September 2021, he served as academic staff and senior research scientist at the Technical University of Ilmenau, Germany, with extensive experience in applied mathematics, numerical optimization, stochastic optimization, control engineering, machine learning, image processing, and computer vision.

Education

  • B.Sc. in Mathematics – Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia
  • M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics – Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia
  • Ph.D. in Numerical Optimization – TU Ilmenau, Germany
