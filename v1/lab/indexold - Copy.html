Autonomous Systems Lab

The Autonomous Systems Lab at AIMS–RIC (African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Research & Innovation Center), Kigali, Rwanda, pioneers research in hybrid AI models, autonomous vehicles, cyber-physical security, and sustainable energy systems.

Lab Philosophy & Vision

We combine rigorous mathematical foundations with practical implementations, aiming to develop autonomous systems that are safe, efficient, sustainable, and ethically designed. Collaboration, innovation, and open scientific communication drive our lab culture, empowering the next generation of researchers globally.
