About

Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu is the German Research Chair at AIMS Rwanda. His research focuses on systems optimization for sustainable resource utilization in Africa, multidisciplinary engineering research, and AI- and data-driven approaches for complex real-world problems.

Education

  • B.Sc. Mathematics – Addis Ababa University
  • M.Sc. Applied Mathematics – Addis Ababa University
  • Ph.D. Numerical Optimization – TU Ilmenau
  • D.Sc. Systems Optimization – TU Ilmenau
