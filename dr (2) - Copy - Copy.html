About

Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu is the AIMS Rwanda German Research Chair based in Kigali, Rwanda. His research focus includes systems optimization for sustainable resource utilization in Africa, multidisciplinary R&D, AI and data-driven solutions for complex problems.

Education

  • B.Sc. Mathematics – Addis Ababa University
  • M.Sc. Applied Mathematics – Addis Ababa University
  • Ph.D. Numerical Optimization – TU Ilmenau
  • D.Sc. Systems Optimization – TU Ilmenau
