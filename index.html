About

Prof. Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu is the German Research Chair at AIMS Rwanda, based in Kigali, Rwanda, and a senior academic leader within the AIMS Research & Innovation Center (RIC), contributing to research on Autonomous and Intelligent Systems.

His current research focuses on systems optimization for sustainable resource utilization in Africa, multidisciplinary research and development for addressing complex engineering problems, and AI- and data-driven approaches for large-scale, uncertain, and real-world systems.

Prior to joining AIMS in September 2021, he served as academic staff and senior research scientist in applied mathematics, numerical optimization, stochastic optimization, control engineering, data-driven optimization, machine learning, image processing, and computer vision, with engineering applications at the Technical University of Ilmenau, Germany.
