About

Prof. Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu is the German Research Chair at AIMS Rwanda, based in Kigali, Rwanda. His current research focus includes systems optimization for sustainable resources utilization in Africa, multidisciplinary research for engineering challenges, and AI- and data-driven approaches for complex problems.

Education

  • B.Sc. Mathematics – Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia
  • M.Sc. Applied Mathematics – Addis Ababa University, Ethiopia
  • Ph.D. Numerical Optimization – TU Ilmenau, Germany
  • D.Sc. Systems Optimization – TU Ilmenau, Germany

Research Interests
