Workshop Overview

An intensive 5-day hands-on workshop covering advanced topics in numerical optimization, Model Predictive Control (MPC), and real-world applications in energy and water systems. This workshop is designed for graduate students, researchers, and professionals seeking to deepen their expertise.

Instructors: Prof. Dr. D.Sc. Abebe Geletu and invited international experts from leading research institutions.

Detailed Curriculum

Day 1-2: Foundations of Optimization
  • Convex and nonconvex optimization fundamentals
  • Gradient-based and gradient-free methods
  • Numerical algorithms and convergence analysis
Day 3-4: Model Predictive Control Theory
  • MPC formulation and implementation
  • Real-time optimization techniques
  • Stability and robustness in MPC systems
Day 5: Applications & Case Studies
  • Energy systems and renewable resources
  • Water networks and sustainable resources
  • Industrial applications and best practices
